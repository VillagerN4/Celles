Selecting a Tile

To select a tile on the map, click on the hex with the left mouse button.

Zooming the Map

To zoom in on the map, hover the mouse over the area you want to zoom into and press the spacebar.

Selecting a Unit

To select a unit, click on the hex where the unit is located with the left mouse button.

Activating a Unit

To activate a unit, press the A key after selecting the unit.

Moving a Unit

To move a unit, select the unit and hover the mouse over the hex you want the unit to move to.
If the movement cost is too high, the unit will stop on the last possible tile.

Attacking with a Unit

To attack, press the C key to have the unit attack all adjacent tiles.

Proceeding to the Next Phase

To proceed to the next phase, press the F key after completing actions in the current phase.
